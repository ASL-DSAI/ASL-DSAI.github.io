Welcome to the Autonomous Simulations Lab, part of the Data Science & AI research ecosystem at IIT Madras in Chennai. We focus on cutting-edge simulation, modelling and autonomous system development to address real-world problems.

About Us

The lab brings together simulation-based research, autonomous navigation, multi-agent systems and AI-driven control to develop solutions for challenging applications in mobility, robotics and smart systems.

Research Areas

  • Autonomous navigation & obstacle avoidance
  • Multi-agent coordination & simulation
  • Intelligent manipulation & robotics in simulated environments
  • Data-driven modelling and AI integration in simulation
